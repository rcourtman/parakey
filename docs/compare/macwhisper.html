Parakey vs MacWhisper

The main difference is workflow. Parakey is for quick push-to-talk text entry at the cursor. MacWhisper is a fuller transcription app for audio files, video, model choice, exports, and long transcripts.

Question Parakey MacWhisper
Primary workflow Hold hotkey, speak, release, paste at cursor. Drop or capture audio/video, transcribe, review, export, and manage transcripts.
Best for Short everyday dictation into existing apps. Meetings, files, long-form transcripts, exports, and richer transcription workflows.
Engine path Parakeet TDT v3 through CoreML on the Apple Neural Engine via FluidAudio. MacWhisper documents multiple model choices, including local and optional cloud-backed options.
Privacy posture No cloud transcription, no telemetry, in-memory transcript history only. MacWhisper documents local transcription for core workflows; check its current model and feature choices before using optional remote services.
Price Free and MIT licensed. MacWhisper has free and paid editions.

Choose Parakey when the job is fast text entry. Choose MacWhisper when the job is transcript production, file handling, export formats, or a broader transcription workspace.

Sources: Parakey source, MacWhisper official site.
