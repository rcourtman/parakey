## What's Changed

- Added **Settings → Language** with auto-detect (default) plus 18
  Latin/Cyrillic-script languages from Parakeet TDT v3. Pinning a
  language biases the decoder against wrong-script bleed-through —
  useful when you only ever dictate in one of those languages.
- Added **Settings → Remove filler words**, an opt-in deterministic
  pass that strips standalone "um", "uh", "ah", "er", "erm", "hm" and
  their elongated forms ("ummm", "uhhh", etc.). Runs after Text
  Corrections so your explicit replacements always win. Off by
  default.
- Updated **Copy Diagnostics** to include the current language and
  filler-removal state.
