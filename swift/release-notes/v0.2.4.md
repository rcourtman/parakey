Reliability and control release. Improves hotkey handling, startup
readiness, microphone selection, cancellation, paste behavior, and
text-correction workflows. No migration steps — `brew upgrade --cask
parakey` or the in-app **Update now…** button handles everything.

## Dictation Reliability

- **Hotkey handling** — suppresses only the configured dictation hotkey,
  handles right-side modifier keys correctly, ignores F-key auto-repeat
  for recording transitions, and keeps the event-tap path responsive.
- **Startup readiness** — starts the audio engine before enabling
  dictation, blocks recording until required permissions are granted,
  and adds retry actions when speech model, audio input, or hotkey
  startup fails.
- **Audio recovery** — restores system audio if Parakey quits during a
  muted recording and refreshes audio input after microphone route
  changes.

## Settings And Workflow

- **Microphone selection** — adds a microphone picker, remembers the
  selected device, falls back cleanly when it is unavailable, and hides
  CoreAudio's default aggregate pseudo-device from the explicit list.
- **Recording control** — adds Escape/menu cancellation for active
  recordings without paste, history, or completion sound.
- **Paste behavior** — lets you append a space, append a newline, or add
  no suffix after pasted dictation.
- **Feedback sounds** — adds a setting to turn start/completion sounds
  off while keeping dictation behavior unchanged.

## Text Corrections And Support

- **Recent transcript actions** — clears in-memory recent transcripts
  and can seed a new correction from the latest transcript without
  logging transcript text.
- **Diagnostics copy** — copies a transcript-free support summary with
  app, permission, microphone, and setting metadata.
- **Correction sharing cleanup** — removes temporary correction-share
  files after the share flow finishes or is dismissed.

## Privacy

- No telemetry, analytics, crash reporting, cloud transcription, new
  Parakey network call, or expanded entitlement was added.
